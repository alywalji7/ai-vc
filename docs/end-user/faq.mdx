# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is AI.VC Platform?
AI.VC Platform is an advanced investment intelligence system that helps venture capital firms and LPs make better investment decisions through data-driven insights.

### Who is AI.VC Platform for?
Our platform is designed for venture capital firms, angel investors, fund managers, and limited partners (LPs) who want to optimize their investment strategies and portfolio management.

### Is my data secure?
Yes, we take data security very seriously. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption methods. We also have strict access controls in place.

## Subscription and Billing

### What subscription plans do you offer?
We offer three tiers:
- Starter: $500/month for 1 seat and basic features
- Pro: $1,000/month for 3 seats and advanced features
- Enterprise: $2,000/month for unlimited seats and all features

### Can I change my subscription plan?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the beginning of the next billing cycle.

### Do you offer a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users to explore the platform before committing to a paid plan.

## Features and Usage

### How do I upload my portfolio?
Navigate to the Portfolio section, click on the Upload button, and select your portfolio file. We support CSV, XLSX, and PDF formats.

### Can I integrate with other tools I use?
Yes, we offer integrations with popular CRM systems, data providers, and financial tools. See the Integrations section for more details.

### How accurate is the AI analysis?
Our AI models are regularly trained on up-to-date investment data. While we strive for high accuracy, we recommend using our insights as one of several factors in your decision-making process.

## Technical Support

### How do I get help if I have an issue?
You can contact our support team by clicking the chat button in the bottom right corner of any page. Our team is available Monday through Friday, 9am-5pm EST.

### Do you offer training sessions?
Yes, we offer onboarding and training sessions for all new customers. Enterprise customers receive additional training and dedicated support.
