# Getting Started with AI.VC Platform

Welcome to the AI.VC Platform! This guide will help you get started with our platform and make the most of its features.

## What is AI.VC Platform?

AI.VC Platform is a comprehensive investment intelligence platform designed to transform complex financial data into actionable insights. It uses sophisticated data ingestion, graph-based analysis, and AI to help venture capital firms and LP investors make better investment decisions.

## Key Features

- **Portfolio Management**: Track and analyze your investment portfolio
- **Deal Flow Radar**: Discover and score potential investment opportunities
- **Due Diligence Automation**: Streamline your due diligence process
- **Investment Committee Simulator**: Test investment theses before committing
- **Term Sheet Generator**: Create and negotiate term sheets efficiently

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Sign up for an account**: Visit the sign-up page to create your account
2. **Set up your profile**: Complete your profile to personalize your experience
3. **Import your portfolio**: Upload your existing investments to get insights
4. **Explore the dashboard**: Familiarize yourself with the main dashboard
5. **Try the tools**: Experiment with our various tools and features

## Need Help?

If you have any questions or need assistance, click the chat button in the bottom right corner of any page to talk to our support team.
