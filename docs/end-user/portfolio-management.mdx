# Portfolio Management

The Portfolio Management module helps you track, analyze, and visualize your investment portfolio.

## Uploading Your Portfolio

1. Navigate to the Portfolio section from the main dashboard
2. Click on the "Upload" button
3. Drag and drop your portfolio file or click to select a file
4. Supported formats: CSV, XLSX, and PDF
5. The system will automatically parse and organize your data

## Viewing Portfolio Details

After uploading your portfolio, you can:

- See a summary of your investments on the Portfolio Dashboard
- View detailed information about individual companies
- Track key performance metrics
- Generate custom reports
- Export data for external analysis

## Data Security

All your portfolio data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard encryption (SSE-KMS) to ensure your data remains secure and private.

## FAQ

### What information should be in my portfolio file?

Your portfolio file should include:
- Company names
- Investment amounts
- Investment dates
- Ownership percentages (if applicable)
- Valuation information (if available)

### Can I update my portfolio?

Yes, you can upload a new file or make manual edits to your existing portfolio data.

### Is there a limit to how many companies I can track?

The number of companies you can track depends on your subscription plan. Please refer to the Billing section for details.
